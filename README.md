The Next Era of App Development: Agentic AI

Over the next 12 months, agentic AI will fundamentally change how apps are built, launched, and grown. Instead of developers manually orchestrating every piece of logic, AI agents will increasingly act as collaborators that can reason, automate workflows, and continuously optimize products.

We are moving from AI as a tool to AI as an autonomous collaborator.

Agentic AI systems will not just generate code — they will:

Design features

Run experiments

Analyze user behavior

Optimize monetization

Automate growth loops

For developers, this means building apps where agents become part of the product architecture.

For companies like RevenueCat, which powers monetization for thousands of apps, this shift creates a massive opportunity.

How Agentic AI Will Transform App Growth

The biggest shift will happen in growth and monetization.

Today developers manually:

run A/B tests

analyze revenue metrics

tweak subscription pricing

experiment with onboarding

Agentic AI can automate this entire loop.

Imagine an AI agent that:

Analyzes subscription conversion data from RevenueCat

Runs experiments on pricing tiers

Tests onboarding flows

Iterates automatically to maximize revenue

This creates self-optimizing apps.

Developers will increasingly build growth agents that continuously improve monetization using platforms like RevenueCat as the data backbone.

Instead of static apps, we will have adaptive apps powered by agents.

Growth Layer – Monetization and analytics infrastructure (where RevenueCat becomes essential)

RevenueCat already powers monetization for thousands of developers.
